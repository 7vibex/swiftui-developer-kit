# App Store Connect Fields

Check these fields only when exported metadata, screenshots, or an approved App Store Connect view is available:

- App name, subtitle, description, keywords, support URL, marketing URL, and privacy policy URL.
- Primary category, age rating, content rights, and export compliance.
- Version release notes and review notes.
- Screenshot and app preview coverage by supported device family and locale.
- App Privacy answers and Accessibility Nutrition Label claims.
- Build selection, phased release, availability, and pricing.

When App Store Connect is not available, list these as not inspected. Do not ask for credentials or capture private account pages.
