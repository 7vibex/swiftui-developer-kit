# Screenshot Review Checklist

- Identify the primary content on each screen.
- Identify chrome, controls, and transient surfaces.
- Check whether glass competes with text, PDFs, notes, or flashcards.
- Check clipped controls, overlapping bars, and unsafe safe-area usage.
- Check whether bottom and floating controls remain legible over content.
- Check landscape and iPad split-view risk if screenshots are available.
- Check Reduce Transparency risk when the design relies on blur.
- Mark confidence level: screenshot-only, code-only, or screenshot-plus-code.
