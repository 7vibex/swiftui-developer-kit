# Liquid Glass Accessibility Checklist

- Check contrast over real content and representative wallpapers.
- Verify readability with Reduce Transparency.
- Verify motion effects with Reduce Motion.
- Avoid placing glass behind long text.
- Ensure icon-only glass controls have labels.
- Keep destructive controls visually stable and unambiguous.
- Confirm focus rings and keyboard focus remain visible.
- Confirm Dynamic Type does not force text into translucent clutter.
- Prefer solid or system material fallbacks when legibility drops.
