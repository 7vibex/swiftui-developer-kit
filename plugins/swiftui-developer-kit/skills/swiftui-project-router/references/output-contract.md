Do not claim that another skill was automatically invoked. A route is a decision, not a defect: use routing risk/status, evidence, next action, verification, confidence, and missing evidence rather than inventing severity. Keep screenshot, build, launch, network, signing, and destructive actions behind the applicable approval boundary.
# Required Quality Fields

For each finding or decision, include severity, evidence, user impact, concrete fix or next action, verification, confidence, and missing evidence. State safety, privacy, approval, and not-inspected boundaries where relevant.
