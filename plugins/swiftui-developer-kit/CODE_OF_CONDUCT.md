# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be useful, respectful, and practical for Apple app developers using Codex. Contributors are expected to participate in a way that is professional and welcoming.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus critique on the work, not the person.
- Assume good intent, but be clear about risks and tradeoffs.
- Keep discussions relevant to the repository.
- Respect privacy and do not share private project data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sexualized, discriminatory, or threatening language.
- Repeated disruption of project discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or block participants who violate this code. Reports should include the relevant context and links when possible.
